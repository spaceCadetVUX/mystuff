04 — FAQ

The common questions.

If yours isn't here, send it over on the contact form — replies within one business day.

What does a typical engagement cost?
Marketing sites start around $6k; SaaS MVPs around $18k. Every project is fixed-price after discovery — no hourly surprises. SEO retainers are optional at $1.5k/mo after launch.
Why Laravel over Next.js / WordPress?
Laravel hits a sweet spot: mature tooling, fast server-rendered HTML (great for SEO and GEO), and a batteries-included admin via Filament. WordPress is fine for content-only sites but caps out fast on custom logic. Next.js is excellent but adds complexity most small businesses don't need.
What exactly is "Generative Engine Optimization"?
SEO optimizes for search engines. GEO optimizes for the LLMs that now sit on top of them — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Claude. It's entity-first writing, clean schema, citeable statistics, and llms.txt. The goal is to be the source an AI cites, not just the link it shows.
Do you work with existing codebases?
Yes — audits and incremental upgrades are about a third of my work. I'll typically start with a two-week audit sprint before committing to longer work, so we both know what we're signing up for.
What does ongoing maintenance look like?
Every build ships with CI, automated tests, Sentry, and uptime monitoring. You can self-host on Forge/Ploi, hand it to an internal team, or retain me for a fixed monthly block.
